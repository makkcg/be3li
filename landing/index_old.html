ABOUT US

Fresh Fruit Group Egypt started by establishing Fresh Fruit Company in 1994 which is specialized in export of agricultural products such as Citrus fruits, Rice, White Beans, Mango, Pomegranate etc… , in the same year Fresh Fruit Center started activity in import of fruits.

In 2004 National Egyptian Commodities started activity in international trade of grains to Eastern Europe & Africa. Fresh Fruit Farms was established in 2005 located in Badr center, Beheira its main crops are Citrus Fruits & Mango.

In 2007 Fresh Fruit established its own packing house for Citrus fruits & Grapes in addition to Hi technology Cold stores.
Fresh Fruit group holds quality certificates:

  • ISO 9001 & HACCP
  • Global GAP
  • BRC
